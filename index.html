About Paul

Hi! I’m Paul, a cadet at the United States Military Academy at West Point.

My mission is to share the love of Christ through photography and videography. In a world overwhelmed by materialism, we often forget the second greatest commandment:

“Love your neighbor as yourself.” – Matthew 22:39

I wouldn’t charge myself for memories—so why should I charge others?
If you need photos or videos, feel free to reach out. If I’m available, I’d love to serve you!
